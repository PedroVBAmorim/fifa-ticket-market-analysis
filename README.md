## Methodology

- Built Excel-based financial models to track cost basis, platform fees, and net returns per position
- Analyzed recently sold listings and historical price trends across multiple marketplaces to identify demand patterns
- Optimized entry and exit timing based on data signals rather than speculation
- Managed platform logistics across multiple secondary-market venues simultaneously

---

## Key Takeaways

- **Early entry outperformed late entry.** Positions opened in Oct 2025 consistently yielded better margins than those entered in early 2026.
- **Knockout stage demand was predictable and strong.** Round of 16 and Quarter-Final tickets appreciated steadily as teams advanced.
- **Ceremonial matches carry more risk.** The 3rd Place game and USA Opener showed that non-elimination fixtures attract less urgency from buyers, compressing resale margins.
- **City and match pairing matters.** A city hosting only high-demand knockout games outperformed a city hosting both profitable and below-target matches.
